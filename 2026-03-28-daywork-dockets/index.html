How would you like to track your budget?

Choose a budget structure that works best for your project. You can change this later.

Recommended

Cost Centres

Group line items into cost centres for a simpler, more streamlined tracking experience. Ideal for teams who want an easier way to monitor spend across categories.

WBS — Work Breakdown Structure

Track your project budget as per the BOQ (Bill of Quantities). Each line item is budgeted individually, giving you granular cost control aligned to your contract.

Day Works

For projects that are day works only. Track labour, plant, and materials on a time and cost basis without a fixed-price contract.

Cost Plus

For projects that are supply plus margin. Track actual costs and apply an agreed margin percentage on top.
