# Delivery Docket — Mobile App

## Problem
Site workers need to capture delivery dockets quickly when trucks arrive. The current process is too complex for on-site use — workers just need to snap a photo and get back to work. Processing can happen later by an admin.

## What changed

- **One-tap capture** — tap the camera button, take a photo of the docket, done
- **Virtual in-tray** — photos go into a queue for admin processing later
- **Confirmation** — instant confirmation that the docket was captured
- **Recent captures** — see what's been captured today at a glance

## Why
The person on site shouldn't be filling in forms. They should capture the docket in 5 seconds and get back to work. An admin or project manager processes the details later from the desktop app.
