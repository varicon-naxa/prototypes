# Bills — List View Redesign

## Problem
The current Bills page has dollar summary cards at the top (Total, Awaiting Credit, Partially Approved, Awaiting Payment, Paid, Overdue) that don't drive action. Users need to quickly see what needs their attention — overdue bills and bills pending their approval.

## What changed

- **Removed** the dollar summary cards across the top — they weren't providing useful at-a-glance information
- **Added overdue bills banner** — prominent red alert at the top showing the count and total dollar value of overdue bills, plus the oldest overdue bill. Clickable to filter to the overdue tab.
- **Added "Pending My Approval" button** — red button in the toolbar with a count badge, giving one-click access to bills that need the current user's approval
- **Added OVERDUE tab** — with a red badge count, making overdue bills a first-class status alongside Draft, Awaiting Approval, etc.
- **Cleaner toolbar** — grouped actions logically: help, entry methods (email/scan/manual), approval filter, and search

## Why
The original page showed dollar totals that didn't drive action. Overdue bills and pending approvals are the two things that need immediate attention — this redesign surfaces them prominently so nothing falls through the cracks.
