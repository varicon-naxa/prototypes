Product Spec — First Login Onboarding Wizard

  • Trigger: Forced on first login. Blocks access to the app until all 6 steps are completed.
  • Step 1 — Accounting Integration: Connect Xero, MYOB, or QuickBooks. Confirmation of Suppliers, Chart of Accounts, and Tax Codes synced shows directly in the selected provider card.
  • Step 2 — PO Settings: Configure purchase order settings with recommended defaults (red ! warning if changed). Invoice email is required and blank by default — must be filled to proceed. Approval workflow requires at least 1 Creator, 1 Approver, and 1 Closer before user can proceed. PDF and Email settings excluded from onboarding (defaults apply).
  • Step 3 — Bills Setup: Configure bill sync settings. Integration start date defaults to 30 days ago. "Sync Bills when" has 3 options.
  • Step 4 — Accounting Codes: Only Expense-type codes. "Archive" checkbox column — archived codes excluded from dropdowns.
  • Step 5 — Resource Cost Sheet: Dropdowns exclude archived codes. "Not Applicable" option. AI auto-fill for same resource type.
  • Step 6 — Tracking Categories (Xero): Instructions + direct link to Xero tracking categories settings.
