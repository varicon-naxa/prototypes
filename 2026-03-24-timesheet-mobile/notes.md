# Timesheet — Mobile Clock In/Out Flow

## Current Flow (from screenshots)
Recreation of the existing Varicon mobile timesheet clock-in/clock-out flow for review and improvement.

## Screens
1. **My Timesheet** — welcome, clock in button, previous timesheets
2. **Clock In** — project selection, GPS location, equipment selection, prestart forms
3. **Dozer Prestart Form** — general info, damage checks, hydraulics, wheels, guards, cabin, electrical, photos, signature
4. **Clocked In** — timer running, equipment and form status shown
5. **Clocked Timesheet** — clock out confirmation with times, break, duration, cost centre assignment, equipment task assignment

## Purpose
Prototype of current flow to identify pain points and areas for improvement.
